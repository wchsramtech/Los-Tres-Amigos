ABOUT US

One day Gavin asked Andon and Mack to come together to decide to start a food truck business because they all wanted to become entrepreneurs at something they were all passionate about. They came up with an agreement to start a taco truck business. Cause they all liked tacos and its something people will buy that is liked and also simple. Our Food truck name is Los Loco Tacos, our tacos are meant to satisfy our customers to their liking.

In addition to our full Taco menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"To bring people together by serving fresh, authentic street tacos." "To create tacos people crave and spaces where everyone feels like they belong, embracing bold flavors and combinations."

CMO: Andon, CFO: Mack and Owner: Gavin

Running hours: Monday to Saturday from 10am to 5pm.

Address: LoanDepot Park in Miami, FL.
